## What's New in Ark v1.0.34

### Transmissions (Scheduled Broadcasts)
- **Prev/Next scroll** — The Scheduled Broadcasts strip has Previous and Next buttons so you can move through event tiles on desktop without relying on horizontal scroll or touchpad.
- **Broadcast card glow** — Live and imminent event cards use a consistent, theme-aligned magenta glow; the glow no longer clips at the edges of the strip.
- **Event location** — Each transmission card now shows **city** (e.g. San Francisco, Boston, Cologne) or **Online** for digital events, with a MapPin or Globe icon.

### Technical
- New `.broadcast-card-glow` utility and strip padding so box-shadow is not clipped in the scroll container.
- Optional `location` field on gaming events (GAMING_EVENTS) with values for all 24 events.
